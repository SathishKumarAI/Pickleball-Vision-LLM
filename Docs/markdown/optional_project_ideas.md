# 🏥 Public EHR Datasets for Risk Prediction (2025)

---

## 1. MIMIC-IV (Medical Information Mart for Intensive Care IV)

**Overview:**  
A publicly accessible database containing de-identified health data from over 40,000 patients admitted to critical care units at the Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center between 2008 and 2019.

**🔗 Link:** [MIMIC-IV v3.1](https://physionet.org/content/mimiciv/3.1/)

**📊 Features:**
- Patient demographics  
- Vital signs  
- Laboratory tests  
- Medications  
- Procedures  
- Diagnoses  
- De-identified clinical notes  

**🔐 Access Requirements:**
- Complete the CITI *Data or Specimens Only Research* course  
- Register for an account on [PhysioNet](https://physionet.org)  
- Submit a data use agreement  

---

## 2. eICU Collaborative Research Database

**Overview:**  
A multi-center ICU database comprising de-identified health data associated with over 200,000 admissions to ICUs across the United States between 2014 and 2015.

**🔗 Link:** [https://eicu-crd.mit.edu/](https://eicu-crd.mit.edu/)

**📊 Features:**
- Vital sign measurements  
- Care plan documentation  
- Severity of illness measures  
- Diagnosis information  
- Treatment details  

**🔐 Access Requirements:**
- Complete the CITI *Data or Specimens Only Research* course  
- Register for an account on [PhysioNet](https://physionet.org)  
- Submit a data use agreement
